Transparency, Built In

A CO2 calculator that won't work without declaring how you account for gas emissions

CO₂ accounting shouldn’t be a black box. As a sustainability consultant, you need full visibility into how emissions are calculated—but just as importantly, you need the ability to define and adapt the rules to fit your clients’ needs.

Our platform gives you both. Every accounting rule is documented, accessible, and fully customizable, so you can create tailored methodologies while maintaining an audit trail of every decision.

Your Rules, Your Methodology

No two clients are the same. Industry standards provide a baseline, but customization is essential—whether it’s adjusting emission factors, handling complex supply chains, or incorporating client-specific data.

Our platform enables:

  • Custom Rule Creation – Define your own formulas, factors, and methodologies in a structured format.

  • Pre-Built Templates – Use industry-standard rules as a starting point and modify them as needed.

  • Full Rule Documentation – Every calculation step is recorded, so your clients always understand the logic behind their carbon footprint.

Instead of forcing you into a rigid system, we provide the tools to shape carbon accounting around real-world complexities.while maintaining a professional look.

Adapting to Changing Regulations

Sustainability reporting isn’t static—regulations evolve, and client requirements shift.

Our system is designed to help you keep up:

  • Versioning & Auditing – Every rule change is logged, so you can track how methodologies evolve over time.

  • Regulatory Adaptability – Easily update rules to align with new compliance standards like CSRD, GHG Protocol updates, or industry-specific mandates.

  • Client-Specific Adjustments – Fine-tune methodologies per client while maintaining consistency across reporting frameworks.

With these capabilities, you stay ahead of regulatory changes without rebuilding calculations from scratch.

Industry-Specific Foundations

To make customization easier, we provide pre-configured rulesets for different industries. These can be used as-is or adapted for client-specific needs:

  • Manufacturing & Logistics – Scope 1 vs. Scope 3 emissions breakdowns, transportation factors.

  • Tech & Services – Cloud energy usage, remote work emissions, digital operations.

  • Retail & Supply Chains – Supplier emissions tracking, lifecycle impact assessments.

These templates give you a head start while keeping full flexibility for deeper customization.
