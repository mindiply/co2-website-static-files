Focus on Insights, Not Data Entry

Trust is built on secure, traceable, complete data. We make it easy(ish).

For carbon accounting to be feasible in smaller organisations, efficiency is key. Businesses and consultants alike struggle with the time-consuming task of data entry, validation, and manual calculations.

Automation That Works for You

Our platform streamlines carbon accounting with automated data imports and real-time calculations. Whether you’re integrating emissions data from invoices, business operations, or supplier reports, the system handles the heavy lifting. Instead of spending hours on spreadsheets, you can concentrate on helping clients reduce their carbon footprint and make informed decisions.

Secure, Flexible, and Continuous

A robust carbon accounting system needs to be secure, adaptable, and always available. Our automation ensures:

  • Security: Your data is handled with strict privacy and compliance measures.

  • Flexibility: Import from various sources and adapt to evolving regulations effortlessly.

  • Continuity: Set up once, and let the platform run in the background, always keeping your records up to date.

  • Traceability: Errors happen, but you want to know that they happened and were reported, and show how you afterwards rectified the situation.

Set Up Once, Run Forever

Carbon accounting shouldn’t be a burden. With a plug-and-play approach, you can get started quickly and trust that your data flows smoothly, accurately, and efficiently—without constant manual intervention. By automating the process, we help sustainability professionals spend less time on data wrangling and more time driving real environmental impact.
